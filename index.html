FAQ

To donate medicines online, you can visit our website and fill out the donation form. We will guide you through the process of donating safely and securely.

We accept donations of unused and unexpired prescription and over-the-counter medicines. Please ensure that the medicines are in good condition and have not expired.

We do not accept donations of expired medicines. Please ensure that all donated medicines are within their expiration dates.
